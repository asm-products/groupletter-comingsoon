Group curated newsletters

{% include '_chunks/_subscribe-form.html' %}

Groupletter allows groups to start a newsletter. The group fills the newsletter by adding links to the backlog and by voting on links already on the backlog. The more votes a link gets the higher the chances that the link ends up in the newsletter (configurable). This keeps the quality of the newsletter high.
